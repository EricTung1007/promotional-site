# AI Coach - General Guide

> Elevate your insurance sales team's practical communication skills through AI-driven simulated customer conversations.

## Platform Introduction
AI Coach is an intelligent training system designed specifically for insurance sales professionals. The platform utilizes advanced AI technology to simulate various realistic customer scenarios, allowing users to safely practice their sales pitches and receive immediate, specific professional feedback.

### Core Values
- **Realistic Customer Interaction** — AI simulates genuine customer reactions based on detailed personas.
- **Multi-stage Sales Training** — Covers the full sales cycle from ice-breaking to closing.
- **Mid-Conversation & Post-Session Evaluations** — Receive line-by-line feedback and a comprehensive report.
- **Personalized Tracking** — Monitor your training progress and growth trajectory via the dashboard.
- **Knowledge Base Assistant** — RAG-powered Q&A feature to look up product info and sales skills.

### Target Audience
| Role              | Use Cases                                           |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Insurance Agents  | Daily sales practice, new product pitch drills, strengthening weaknesses |
| New Hires         | Onboarding training, first-contact simulations, building confidence |
| Team Managers     | Monitoring team practice data, managing content, tweaking AI behavior |
| Instructors       | Designing practice scenarios, managing grading rubrics, optimizing prompts |
