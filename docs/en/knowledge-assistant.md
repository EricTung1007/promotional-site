# Knowledge Assistant

> Look up sales knowledge anytime as professional support for your practice.

## Overview
The Knowledge Assistant is a built-in intelligent Q&A feature in AI Coach, based on RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) technology. It retrieves relevant information from the company's knowledge base to generate accurate answers. You can use it anytime before, during, or after practice.

## How to Open
Click the **Knowledge Assistant Icon** in the top right navigation bar.

> This feature requires knowledge base read permissions. If you do not see the icon, please contact your administrator.

---

## Interface Guide

### Title Bar
- **AI Logo**: Identification icon for the Knowledge Assistant
- **AI Chat**: Title text
- **Status**: Green dot indicates "Online"; red dot indicates "Offline"
- **Clear Chat**: Clear all chat history and start over

### Chat Area
- **Welcome Screen**: Displayed when first opened, providing usage guidance
- **Message List**: Your questions (right) and AI responses (left)
- **Typing Indicator**: Animation shown while AI is generating an answer

### Input Area
- Input Box: Enter the question you want to look up
- Send Button: Click to send the question (temporarily disabled while AI is answering)

---

## How to Use
1. Click the Knowledge Assistant icon to open the chat window.
2. Type your question in the input box.
3. Click Send or press Enter.
4. Wait for the AI to retrieve data from the knowledge base and respond.
5. Review the answer and ask further questions if needed.

## Use Cases

| Scenario | Example Question |
| --- | --- |
| Product Specs | "What is the coverage of Insurance XX?" |
| Script Ref | "Advice on scripts for customers worried about high premiums?" |
| Policy Lookup | "What is the claims application process?" |
| Comparison | "What is the difference between Plan A and Plan B?" |
| FAQs | "What are the top 3 most common customer questions?" |

## Important Notes
- The quality of the answers depends on the knowledge base content uploaded by the administrator.
- Answers are for reference only. Please rely on official company documents in actual sales scenarios.
- Chat history is stored locally in your browser.
- Switching accounts or clearing the chat will reset the history.
